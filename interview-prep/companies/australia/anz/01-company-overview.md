## üöÄ Digital Transformation Journey

### ANZ Plus Platform

**Launch:** 2022  
**Vision:** Complete digital banking replatforming

**Key Features:**
- Modern, mobile-first experience
- Real-time insights and analytics
- Simplified user interface
- Integrated financial management

**Technology:**
- Built on **Fabric** (proprietary digital banking platform)
- Microservices architecture
- Cloud-native design
- Regular, rapid updates

**Timeline:** Consolidate to one digital frontend and one core banking system by Sept 2027

### Digital Backbone (Institutional)

**Launch:** 2021

**Purpose:** Technological "spine" for institutional business

**Architecture:**
- Connected platforms producing/consuming standardized data
- Growing data ecosystem
- Common customer identifier
- Internal availability at scale

**Requirements for New Initiatives:**
- Must connect to Digital Backbone
- Meet data production standards
- Use common identifiers
