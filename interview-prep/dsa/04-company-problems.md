**Focus Areas:**
1. System Design (40% of prep time)
2. Advanced DP & Graphs (30%)
3. Design Patterns & Code Quality (20%)
4. Behavioral (10%)

**Must-Know Problems:**
- LRU/LFU Cache (demonstrate design skills)
- Word Ladder II (BFS optimization)
- Median of Two Sorted Arrays (binary search mastery)
- Serialize/Deserialize (tree mastery)
- Critical Connections (advanced graphs)

### Staff/Principal Engineers

**Focus Areas:**
1. System Design (50%)
2. Leadership/Behavioral (25%)
3. Complex DSA (15%)
4. Architecture patterns (10%)

**Must-Know Problems:**
- Design problems (Twitter, URL Shortener, etc.)
- Graph problems with complex constraints
- Problems requiring multiple optimal solutions
